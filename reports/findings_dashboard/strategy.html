Pattern

  • Formation length is 3 to 6 bars from mother bar to signal bar.
  • Bar 2 must stay inside or equal to the mother bar range.
  • Bullish signal breaks below the mother low, then closes back inside the mother range in the upper third of its own bar.
  • Bearish signal breaks above the mother high, then closes back inside the mother range in the lower third of its own bar.

Legacy Context

  • Signal forms around the 20/50 SMA retracement zone.
  • A 50 SMA anchor exists before the mother bar.
  • Impulse from the anchor is at least 1.5 ATR.
  • Trend-side ratio, anchor efficiency, pre-mother retrace, and recent progress gates must pass.

Trade Model

  • ATR stop model only for the baseline.
  • Target is 1.5R.
  • Next-bar open entry if risk is at most 1.25 ATR and still inside the mother range.
  • If both stop and target are touched in the same bar, stop gets priority.

Valid Cases

Valid examples have both parts: the Force Strike pattern and the legacy trend/retracement context.

Invalid Cases

Invalid examples can have a nice-looking candle pattern, but fail because the structure, context, or sequence is wrong.

Valid Bullish Force Strike

VALID
  • Prior trend and SMA context agree with long side.
  • Price retraces into the 20/50 zone before the mother bar.
  • Signal only breaks the mother low, then closes bullish inside the mother range.

Valid Bearish Force Strike

VALID
  • Prior trend and SMA context agree with short side.
  • Signal only breaks the mother high.
  • Signal closes bearish inside the mother range.

Bar 2 Is Not Inside Mother

INVALID
  • The first baby bar must be inside or equal to the mother range.
  • If bar 2 breaks outside, the setup is rejected before entry logic matters.

Two-Sided Mother Break

INVALID
  • A valid Force Strike is one-sided before the signal close.
  • If both mother extremes break, the setup is treated as noisy and discarded.

No 20/50 Retracement Context

INVALID
  • The pattern alone is not enough.
  • The structure must overlap or sit close enough to the SMA retracement zone.

Weak Or Stale Trend

INVALID
  • There must be a usable 50 SMA anchor and directional impulse.
  • Flat, stale, or low-efficiency price action should fail the context gate.
